# Non-claims Checklist

The submission materials preserve these non-claims:

- No public GitHub Release.
- No Zenodo DOI.
- No production readiness.
- No FAIR Digital Object Framework conformance.
- No FAIR Digital Object Framework certification, endorsement, or deployment.
- No legal compliance.
- No semantic correctness of AI output.
- No ZKP implementation.
- No full repository pytest success.
- No publication acceptance.
- No artifact badge.
- No external endorsement.
- No submitted, accepted, reviewed, or published status.
- No passport-type photograph in Git.
- No private phone number or full postal address in Git.
