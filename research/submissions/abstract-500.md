## Abstract / 摘要

**English**  AI runtime traces are useful for debugging, but they do not automatically become portable evidence objects. Execution Evidence Object is proposed as a standards specimen that gives AI runtime evidence an object form with identity, steps, context, and integrity hashes. The repository already includes a schema, example objects, a verification script, a human-readable demo, cross-framework export prototypes, and an FDO-style wrapper example. The contribution is not formal adoption. The contribution is a reproducible specimen for discussing execution evidence as a digital object.
