## Not Blockers For Pre-Review

- Real LangChain runtime integration.
- OpenTelemetry Collector integration.
- Third valid trace.
- GitHub release.
- Zenodo DOI.

These are not required for pre-review because the current question is whether
the manuscript's bounded method contribution is clear and credible. They may
become required before final submission depending on venue expectations and
external reviewer feedback.
