# Contributor Credit Policy for Paid External Review

Date: 2026-06-01

## Default Credit

Code, tests, documentation, and fixture contributions are credited through the
public Git commit and pull request history. Review comments and reproduction
reports are credited through public GitHub issues and discussions where they
occur.

## Acknowledgements

Substantial external review feedback may be acknowledged in project materials
where appropriate. Examples include:

- a reproducibility review that identifies actionable installation or command
  issues;
- a domain review that materially improves the claim boundary;
- a documentation review that prevents unsupported claims.

Acknowledgement does not imply authorship, endorsement, or responsibility for
the full project.

## Authorship

Authorship is not promised for paid review, paid issue filing, or small paid
PRs. Authorship may be considered only when a contributor makes a substantial
intellectual or software contribution, agrees to be listed, and accepts
responsibility for the relevant work.

Purely financial support, superficial review, generic praise, administrative
help, or issue triage alone does not create authorship.

## Disclosure

If paid contributions materially affect documentation, tests, or manuscript
materials, disclose the paid external contribution where relevant. Do not hide
financial relationships that could be perceived as affecting the work.
