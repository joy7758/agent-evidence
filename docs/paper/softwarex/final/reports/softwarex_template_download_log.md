## SoftwareX Requirements Recorded

The official SoftwareX guide states that submissions consist of two major parts: a short descriptive paper with a 3000-word limit and an open-source software distribution with support material. The guide also states that submissions are accepted only when they follow SoftwareX journal-specific templates.
