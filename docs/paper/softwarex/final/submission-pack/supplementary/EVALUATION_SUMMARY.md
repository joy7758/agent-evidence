AEP-Media uses a bounded conformance-oriented evaluation.

Matrix | Cases | Expected outcome
--- | ---: | ---

## Interpretation

The evaluation supports local conformance, diagnosable failures, offline bundle verification, strict declared time-trace validation, adapter fixture ingestion, and optional-tool reporting.

It does not support claims of real external verification, legal admissibility, non-repudiation, trusted timestamping, production deployment, or complete forensic sufficiency.
